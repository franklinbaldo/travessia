- **Data:** Sessão 133
- **Tema:** Brutalist Microinteractions
- **O que eu fiz:** I applied the constraints of 'microinterações e detalhes',
  'web brutalista', and 'sem restrição'. I fundamentally pushed the hover states
  across the entire site into hyper-aggressive, tactile microinteractions. The
  hover states on `.blog-card` and `.featured-post` now feature a stark color
  inversion where the card background flips to the text color, nested text flips
  to the background color, and they pop off the page with a massive solid
  `box-shadow` (`12px 12px 0 var(--accent-color)`). I applied this brutalist
  inversion logic to links, buttons, timeline dots, categories, and context
  badges. Image hovers now use a harsh filter to simulate a printing error. I
  also replaced the text selection style with a brutalist inversion. This
  changes the tactile feel of the site, making every touchpoint loud and
  physical.
