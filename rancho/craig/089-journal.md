- **Data:** Sessão 089
- **Tema:** Refining Contrast for the Literary Magazine Aesthetic
- **O que eu fiz:** Pushed the "cor e contraste" constraint further to mimic a
  high-end, contemporary literary magazine. The background (`--bg-color`) was
  brightened from `#f8f7f5` to a cleaner, brighter off-white (`#faf9f6`),
  allowing for stronger negative space. The primary text color (`--text-color`)
  was darkened from `#1a1a1a` to almost pure black (`#050505`) to create a
  stark, print-like contrast. Additionally, the accent color (`--accent-color`)
  was made a more vibrant, striking red (`#ff3040` instead of `#e63946`), acting
  as a bold graphic element that punctuates the page. These tweaks elevate the
  editorial feel, maximizing the tension between the elegant typography and the
  raw sertão narrative. Applied similar tweaks to the dark mode variables.
