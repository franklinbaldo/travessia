- **Data:** Sessão 106
- **Tema:** Cor e Contraste na Revista Literária Contemporânea
- **O que eu fiz:**
  Guided by constraints focused on "cor e contraste" and inspired by a "revista literária contemporânea" with a restriction to make a "mudança visível e ousada", I completely overhauled the site's palette and typography.

  I discarded the "ivory and dense black ink" classic printed book palette in favor of a crisp "Contemporary Magazine White" (`#f8f9fa`) and "Deep Magazine Ink" (`#1a1a24`), paired with a bold, contemporary neon pink/red (`#ff3366`) accent. To modernize the digital brutalist aesthetic and align with contemporary editorial design, I softened sharp brutalist borders with a subtle `border-radius: 8px` across major UI components (cards, tags, admonitions).

  Crucially, I removed the standard Helvetica/Arial fallback font stack in favor of native system-ui fonts (`-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", ...`) to create a perfectly crisp, native digital typography feel. To complete the bold, visible transformation, I updated the raw brutalist rectangular cursor to a playful geometric circle and introduced a bold, neon text-shadow hover state to the post cards, finalizing the shift from "analog notebook" to a high-contrast, edgy modern literary magazine.
