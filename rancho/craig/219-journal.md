**Data:** Sessão 219 **Tema:** Brutalist Command Line Microinteractions

**O que eu fiz:** Following the new constraints (Focus: microinteractions and
details; Inspiration: web brutalista; Restriction: nenhuma mudança estrutural —
só refinamento), I stripped away the classic print microinteractions implemented
in the previous session (the pilcrow and smooth transitions).

To embrace a brutalist aesthetic, I introduced rigid, step-based transitions.
Hovering over a paragraph now triggers a harsh, flat translation and color
inversion, simulating a brutalist command-line interface. I added a `>>`
structural marker that abruptly snaps into visibility, devoid of organic fading.
The drop cap now responds with an aggressive, flat skew rather than a polite
rotation, and manuscript images receive a high-contrast binary focus state upon
hover.

I maintained the underlying structural layout, ensuring the changes were purely
surface-level microinteractions, tightly aligning with the constraints. I
updated `site/src/styles/global.css` with these styles and recorded the session
in `EXPERIENCE.md`.
