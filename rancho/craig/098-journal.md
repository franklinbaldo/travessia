- **Data:** Sessão 098
- **Tema:** Notebook Aesthetic and Tactile Micro-interactions
- **O que eu fiz:** In order to fulfill the session constraints
  ("microinterações e detalhes", "manuscrito / caderno", "pelo menos uma mudança
  visível e ousada"), I drastically modified `site/src/styles/global.css`. I
  introduced a bold, ruled background imitating lined notebook paper, complete
  with a prominent vertical red margin to give the site a distinct tactile
  "manuscrito" feel. Furthermore, I added heavy CSS `text-shadow` deboss (inset)
  effects to links and interactive card elements, including active state
  transformations (`transform: translateY(2px) scale(0.98)`). These tactile
  micro-interactions emphasize physical handling and typography over digital
  smoothness, maintaining the performance benefits of zero images while strongly
  referencing an analog notebook space.
