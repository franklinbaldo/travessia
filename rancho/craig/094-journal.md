- **Data:** Sessão 094
- **Tema:** Web Brutalista Typographic Refinement
- **O que eu fiz:** To adhere to the constraints focusing on "tipografia e espaçamento" and the inspiration of "web brutalista" without making structural DOM changes, I overhauled the `site/src/styles/global.css` typography rules. I abandoned the classic printed book aesthetics by stripping out `Georgia` serif font and `text-indent: 2rem;` paragraph rules. Instead, I established a harsh, digital-native sans-serif default (`"Helvetica Neue", "Arial", sans-serif`) with raw, left-aligned text spaced explicitly via `margin-bottom: 1.5rem;`. I changed meta text to a brutalist monospace (`"Courier New", Courier, monospace`). Finally, I intensified the main container's presence, increasing its border to `6px` and its solid unblurred offset shadow to `16px`. The result is a textually aggressive reading experience natively adapted to the web.
