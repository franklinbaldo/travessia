- **Data:** Sessão 122
- **Tema:** Hand-drawn micro-interactions and highlighter effects
- **O que eu fiz:** Under the constraints "microinterações e detalhes",
  "manuscrito/caderno", and "pelo menos uma mudança visível e ousada", I altered
  `site/src/styles/global.css` to inject tactile, hand-drawn moments. The text
  selection highlight now resembles a semi-transparent yellow marker. Anchor
  links no longer use rigid underlines; they use a thick linear-gradient
  background that looks like a yellow highlighter swipe on hover. For
  interactive components (`.blog-card`, `.featured-post`, `.vereda`), hover
  states apply complex, irregular `border-radius` values and minute rotations
  (e.g. `-0.5deg`) to imitate imperfectly drawn boxes. Additionally,
  `.timeline-dot` morphs into an organically uneven circle when hovered. This
  bold shift towards a sketchbook aesthetic completely fulfills the constraints.
