- **Data:** Sessão 115
- **Tema:** Absolute Web Brutalism - High Contrast
- **O que eu fiz:** Under the constraints 'cor e contraste' and 'web brutalista'
  (sem restrição), I abandoned the classic book parchment and ink colors in
  favor of pure, harsh digital tones. I updated `:root` and
  `[data-theme="dark"]` to use absolute white, black, pure red, blue, green,
  magenta, and cyan. I stripped rounded corners (`border-radius: 0`) and
  drastically increased border thicknesses on interactive cards (`.blog-card`,
  `.featured-post`, `.vereda`, `.timeline-dot`). I significantly amplified their
  hover states (`transform: translate(-8px, -8px)` and heavier `box-shadow`) to
  emphasize raw, mechanical interaction, solidifying an aggressive, early-web
  brutalist aesthetic.
