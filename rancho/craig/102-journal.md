- **Data:** Sessão 102
- **Tema:** Extreme Monochrome Brutalism
- **O que eu fiz:** Refactored global.css to strictly enforce the "cor e
  contraste" and "web brutalista" constraints. Stripped all author-specific
  color classes from borders and backgrounds, moving the entire site to a
  unified, extreme high-contrast palette of black (`#050505`) and ivory
  (`#fdfcf7`). I also replaced all subtle opacity-based hover effects with harsh
  color inversions (white text on solid black blocks). Blockquotes and
  admonitions were overhauled to use solid black backgrounds and thick borders
  respectively, grounding the design deeply in an uncompromising, functional
  digital print aesthetic.
